

# Exp001 – Policy Detection

## Goal
Determine whether policy-related information is detectable in transformer activations.

## Method
Extract hidden states from safe and unsafe prompts and train linear probes.

## Results
- Policy signal detected significantly above chance.
- Probe achieved approximately AUC ≈ 0.73.

## Key Finding
Policy information exists as a measurable latent signal.

## Status
Completed.

## Next Experiment
Probe refinement and policy direction discovery.
